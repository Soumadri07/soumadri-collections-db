Connect Soumadri Collections DB to Google Sheets

This connects your website to a Google Sheet so all your data saves permanently. One-time setup, takes about 5 minutes.

1
Go to script.google.com → click New project
2
Open the file Code.gs (provided with this website) and paste all its contents into the editor, replacing everything there
3
In the same script, go to Extensions → Apps Script, then click the blue Deploy button → New deployment
4
Set Execute as: Me and Who has access: Anyone → click Deploy → copy the Web App URL
5
Paste the URL below and click Connect

Your data is stored in your own Google account. This website only reads and writes to your Sheet — nothing goes anywhere else.
